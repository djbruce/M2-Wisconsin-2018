  • Air Travel: The Dane County Regional Airport (MSN) is located roughly five miles from downtown Madison. Those flying into the Dane County airpot should take a taxi to the hotel. (The fare should be roughly $20-$25.)

  • Ground Trave: It is often cheaper and more convient to fly into O'Hare International Airport (ORD) in Chicago, IL or into General Mitchell International Airport (MKE) in Milwaukee, WI and then take a bus to Madison. From O'Hare one can take the Van Galder bus, and from Mitchell one can take the Badger Bus. Both of these have stops at the Memorial Union on UW's campus, blocks away from the Lowell Center Hotel.

  • Campus Travel: Conference activities will all take place in Van Vleck Hall located at the top of Bascom Hall. From the hotel it is a short walk to campus. (Those wishing to avoid walking up Bascom Hill can should consider going down university and through the Botany Gardens.) A campus map can be found here.
